Sustainable Vegetable Stock

Vegetable Stock made from Scraps

Help combate food waste by utalizing all of your leftover vegetables

Ingredients:

  • Frozen leftover vegetable scraps
  • Water
  • Fresh/dried herbs of choice
  • Salt & Pepper corns to taste

Steps:

  1. Place veggie scraps in 10 quart pot (add additional veggies if necessary)
  2. Fill pot with just enough water to cover vegetables
  3. Add salt, pepper corns, and herbs to water
  4. Bring to boil and simmer for up to 12 hours
  5. Cool and store as desired
