French Toast

A classic french breakfast that is quick and simple to make and garunteed to be a family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • Day old bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla or Maple syrup
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Beat together eggs, vanilla or syrup, and milk in a mixing bowl.
  2. Heat pan or skillet with butter to medium high heat
  3. Dip bread into egg mixture and tranfer to pan
  4. Cook until golden brown on both sides
  5. Top with fresh fruit or toppings of choice and enjoy!
