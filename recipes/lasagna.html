Lasagna

World's Best Lasagna with Meat Sauce

A savory classic italian dish with from scratch pasta sauce, ricotta cheese, and an italian meat blend.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb italian sweet sausauge
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Pasta sauce
  • Ricotta cheese - 16oz
  • 1 egg
  • Lasagna noodes

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat
  2. Add sauce to meat and spices
  3. Cook lasagna and drain
  4. Combine egg and cheese with salt and parsley
  5. Preheat oven
  6. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in bottom of baking pan. Layer on noodles, ricotta, cheese and repeat.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes
